NICE guidelines 2008:

Pre-op: Temp must be &gt; 36° before anaesthesia

Intra-op:

Monitor temp if:

o surgery &gt;30min

o temp &lt;36°

o ASA &gt;2

o GA+RA

Fluid warmer if:

\- &gt;500ml fluid

Post-op: warm if &lt;36°
